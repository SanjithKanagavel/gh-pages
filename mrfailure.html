MR.FAILURE
November 22, 2015 Me : Oh, Mr.Success where are you ? I tried hard finding you Mr.Success. Where are you hiding ? Am I not capable of finding you? Am I into wrong room?  Mr.Failure : No Buddy, you are capable but you are missing something ! Go find it.  Mr.Success : Hey you, I am just at your doorstep. Find the keys. Let me in. 

"Failures are the spare keys for reaching success"

Yes,they help you to reach success. They always give you some hint about the your mistakes that led to failure.
Oh, Mistakes ?  I did it perfectly !! How can it fail then ?
No one is perfect in this world. We tend to make mistakes. It's a part of our life. Only Some people would try to find those mistakes and correct them. But most of them quit. You are among that some not the most.
"Mastering things can overcome failure"
Edison failed more than 1000 times to invent light bulbs. Learn from failures. Believe every failure would teach you something new every time.
"Try again never stop it"
Mr.Failure has a habit of testing your patience. If you pass it out with a warm smile he would allow you visit Mr.Success or else you will never find the keys of door.
"Take it as a Pride"
Never care for others when you are failed because others wouldn't have any opportunity to meet Mr.Failure. Tell them he gave a spare keys to reach Mr.Success. Don't ever share it with others. Feel happy and blessed for a failure.
"Motivate Yourself"
Listen to motivational videos or read motivational books when you are stressed and felt bad about failures. Mr.Failure is your coaching master who will make you stronger. Don't feel sad for having him. You are blessed to have him.
"Imagine your visit with Mr.Success"
Imagination is a secret power to change things or even the world. Imagine about you meet Mr.Success and you are happy for him. When you do it so you can feel that things would change accordingly making a way to reach Mr.Success. Mr.Failure will teach you more than Mr.Success does. So, always look forward to meet Mr.Failure. He will introduce to his friend Mr.Success ! Hope you would meet Mr.Success soon !
