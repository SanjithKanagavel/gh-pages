Review on Mr.Green - The Jumper game
Android / iOS Game
When you think of endless running games you probably think Temple Run and Run Like Hell. Flappy Bird was an endless runner too, well in the essence of not hitting objects. Mr Green the Jumper is a more interesting take on the Flappy Bird formula. It is minimalist in style so don’t worry about using this one to show off your phone’s power. Mr Green is interesting in its use of shadows and lighting and moving objects. These are all things that Flappy Bird didn’t have. Of course, Flappy Bird featured rather prominently nods to classics such as Super Mario Bros on the NES (the pipes) and other 8-Bit games.
