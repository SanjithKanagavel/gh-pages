SHE IS BEYOND COMPARE
May 8, 2016

She is all above comparisons.

She is the first one who wished me in this world. She is the first one who brought me into this world with expectations making me the best in this world. She is the one who loved me even before I was actually alive. She had me inside her for 9 months. From all struggles she finally gave me birth and showed me that even life exist outside her. She is my first trustable source who introduced me to Daddy.

She had sleepless nights when I was sick. She dressed me with most beautiful dresses every day to make me the beautifulest child. She sacrificed her career to take care of me all time. She was the one who accompanied me most of the time. She took me to all the places where I would like to enjoy. She carried me with her all time till I started walking. She showed me how to talk and felt like she was in world's highest top peak when I had my first teething. Felt like she was up above all the universes when I had my first talk.

She is the one who waited in a long queue for admission in best school in town. I could still remember the first cry we had when during my first schooling days. She is the one who waits at gates before the school bell rings. She is the one who remembers and prays all time for me. She is the one who first taught me how to speak,pronounce and learn new things. She is the one who bought me all things that I wished I could have. She spent her money without any hesitation just to find a smile in my face.

She makes and keeps me happy. She is the one who prides about me every time to whom ever she talks. She helped me taking every important decisions of life. She's been a great role model to me. Every second she thinks in making my life better. She sacrificed most of her important aspects for me. She is the one who will always be with me during correct and wrong decisions. No one can replace her feelings and care towards me. Nothing seems to be possible without her. Things I have right now is all because of her dedication and sacrifices that she possessed. She is the best in this world.
She is a precious gift in my life and she is my MOM. We have more way to go. More pride to earn. Stay with me all time. 

LOVE YOU MOM :)

With words from heart, Proud Son Sanjith.
