IT'S LITTLE GOOD
April 17, 2016 As the quote says "Every action has equal and opposite reaction", every bit of my small act curves my life stone.

"If I am happy it reflects in a positive way.

If I am sad it kills my time by making me one waste piece on earth.

If I am brave of what I am doing I get success on whatever roads I take.

If I fear them I fail at every small ones."
I learnt something today, it's all about taking life as it goes but do your best to reap success at each turns you take. Even I am in hateful situation right now, I am going to tell myself that I wished for this situation so that I could find things that I hate and I going to thank god for this situation. If didn't happen I would have not known what I am upto and where my life has taken me.

So I am thankful to the situation right now. I believe the best in yet to come and it's on the way.

C'mon its not bad,it's little good.

I am waiting.
