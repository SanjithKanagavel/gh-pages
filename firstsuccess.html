FIRST SUCCESS
November 22, 2015 Are you the one filled with visionary dreams?  Are them dreams till now?  Still you live in a dreamy world?  Not finding ways to achieve them? Are you the only one building your crazy dream ? No one believes your dream ? Did I hear yes? Don't worry everything will change after your FIRST SUCCESS What is First Success ?
"People will trust you after your first success"
No one believes you until first success of your dream. Because they will not have that vision or they will not know that it's possible until you prove them. But it's the most difficult part of making your dream come true. But's it's possible through a secret formula.

BELIEF + GUTS + HARDWORK = FIRST SUCCESS

There are times you would wrongly decide that your dreams are only dreams, at those times you should be energized through motivation. You are the creator of your dream. Your the only chance to make it happen  and impact people's life. You should self motivate yourself. "There are rough times that are gonna come. They have not come to stay,but they have come to pass." Just believe and chase it.

"You are the creator of your own history"
Dreams are visions of reality but it needs Guts to chase them.  You need guts to follow your dream even entire world is against you. "If you have no guts in you then you got no story and no glory."

"All you need in life is 20 seconds of insane courage"

Nothing can be achieved without hard work. Work, work, ever work, is a great panacea. Just keep keep working and forever until your dream is a reality. Be passionate. Passionate work is an essential oxygen for success.

"Hardwork is the secret of success"

Give equal weight to the key factors of your first success. Because it is necessary to the equation.

Once you got your first success then SNOWBALL EFFECT happens.

What is Snowball Effect ?

Just imagine of rolling down a small snowball from top of snow mountain. What would happen ? The small snowball size get increases as it rolls down and finally it comes as huge snowball when it reaches bottom.

"Success has a snow ball effect"

Now you can relate small snowball with first success. When it finally reaches your dream's reality it would be a Life changing Success.

You create your world. Keep dreaming and make them reality.

Everything is possible. You can do it.

Chant the below quote for seven times and feel motivated.

"I know"

"I Can"

"I Will"
