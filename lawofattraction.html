LAW OF ATTRACTION
February 18, 2016
"Whatever the mind can conceive and believe it can acheive" - Napolean Hill
Our life is filled with experiences. When things are happening smoothly we are happy but in difficult situation we seem to throw tension and complain how unlucky we are. If we observe carefully it happened because we expected them to. This is called Law of Attraction. Positive thought attracts better things or if thoughts were negative it could make the day even worse.
"You create your own universe as you go along" – Winston Churchill
Most of us don't achieve what we really want and always feel that we are limited to what things we can get in life. Aside from not feeling good about ourselves, we don't actually believe in ourselves. We don't believe that we can achieve whatever we want in life and that it is impossible. If a person looks at the bright side and believes that nothing is impossible he will achieve what he really wants.
"To bring anything into your life, image that it already there" - Richard Bach
There are three simple steps that can help a person achieve, these steps are to ask, believe, and receive. A person should ask him what he really wants and then he should believe in it and feel that they will get it or actually they already have that thing he wants and then eventually with time that person will receive it. But the most important of these steps is to do them positively.
"Believe that you can make it all happen for you"
You're meant to have everything you desire or love. You were not born to struggle. You were meant to be victorious. All you need is a positive thought that could change your life. You have an Amazing life. You're Awesome.
