LITTLE ONE MATTERS MOST
August 8, 2016

Every objects in this world are made up of tiny atoms. Tiny drops of water makes a mighty ocean. Life of human being starts inside a tiny sperm. Construction of world highest skyscraper starts with tiny brick laid first. That’s where everything starts.

Give importance to every tiny things in life. Respect them as if they were going to change the way of your life. Impact of little things will make you even believe that failure or success is just a second in the clock of life. Little characteristics like responsibility, patience, empathy, courtesy, tolerance impacts lot for a person. It helps him get a job, earns respects among fellow co-workers.

Be thankful to little things like to your mom who made breakfast, cab drive who dropped you safely in office, work you have right now or even to this author who inspired you someway. Being thankful attracts more little things that would make your life better.

Start your day with little things like waking up early in morning, performing meditation, writing daily blog post, jogging or cycling, or some little thing that you might give a new inner happiness. Doing them will enrich your regular work and keep you motivating throughout the day.

Summation : Life is made up of things that matters and they aren’t big they are so tinier than you ever imagine. Even challenging or impossible work is a tiny dot in the circle of life for those who believe their ambition or goal they chase isn’t a tiny one.
